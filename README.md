# NetGuard Home
### Your Home WiFi is Leaking Data. We’re Here to Plug the Holes.
NetGuard Home provides privacy shields that block trackers and ads across every device in your house—from Smart TVs to tablets.

### Your Smart TV, doorbell, and fridge are likely "phoning home" to servers in unknown locations every single minute.
### Up to 30% of your internet bandwidth is wasted downloading ads and tracking scripts you never asked for.
### Browser ad-blockers only work on your computer. They can't protect your phone apps, gaming consoles, or IoT devices.

## How NetGuard Works
### We provide a custom-configured NetGuard Shield (a tiny, silent device) that plugs into your router.
### The Shield acts as a "bouncer" for your internet, automatically blocking known ad-servers and data-harvesters.
### Your internet feels faster, your devices stay private, and those annoying "sponsored" interruptions disappear.
